Join Us as a Campus Ambassador!

About the Program

KARMIKH is looking for enthusiastic and driven students to join our team as Campus Ambassadors!

Responsibilities:

  • Promote Karmikh and its products on campus through various channels (e.g., social media, events, workshops).
  • Host and organize online and offline events to bring awareness about Karmikh's products and brand.
  • Provide feedback and insights on student needs and preferences.
  • Spread the word about competitions, training opportunities, scholarships, and other events.
  • Build relationships on campus with faculty and student groups.
  • Test products and features.
  • Promote product use and new product launches to students.

Requirements:

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong leadership and organizational abilities.
  • Passion for technology and innovation.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Proficiency in social media and digital marketing.
  • Experience in the management field is preferred.

Eligibility:

  • Current students enrolled in a recognized university or college.
  • All courses and specializations are welcome.
  • Preferably be a second or third-year student at your university.
  • Preferably pursuing a BA/BSc, Management, STEM degree, BTECH, or any other relevant degree.
  • Able to plan and coordinate for brand events at the campus.
  • Actively involved in student life on campus.

Selection Procedure:

  • Registration.
  • Assignment Screening.
  • Interview Call.
  • Offer Letter.

Incentives:

  • Opportunity to gain valuable work experience.
  • Certificate of Completion.
  • Letter of Recommendation (upon successful completion of the program).
  • Opportunity to transition to Full-Time Role.
  • Opportunity to earn stipend up to 10K based on sales.

Skills Required:

  • English Proficiency (Spoken).
  • English Proficiency (Written).
  • Event Management.
